Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping at EliteWear.

Orders

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account dashboard and viewing your recent orders. International shipments may have limited tracking depending on the destination.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact our concierge team immediately at concierge@elitewear.com if you need to make changes.

To cancel an order, log into your account and navigate to your orders. If the order is still within the 2-hour window, you will see a cancel option. Alternatively, contact our support team with your order number for assistance.

Shipping

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. International orders typically arrive within 7-14 business days depending on customs clearance. All orders are processed within 24 hours.

We offer complimentary express shipping on all orders over $500. Standard shipping is $15, and express shipping is $25. International shipping costs vary by destination and are calculated at checkout.

Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees applicable in their country.

International shipments include tracking that can be monitored through your account dashboard. Tracking updates may be less frequent once the package leaves the originating country. Contact our support team if you have concerns about your shipment.

Returns

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original condition with all tags attached. Final sale items, intimates, and personalized pieces cannot be returned. We provide a prepaid return label for domestic orders.

Log into your account, go to your orders, and select the item you wish to return. Follow the return instructions to generate a return label. Pack the item securely with all tags and include the return form. Drop off at any designated carrier location.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. The amount will be credited to your original payment method. International returns may take longer due to customs processing.

We offer exchanges for sizes only. To exchange, please initiate a return and place a new order for the desired size. This ensures the quickest processing. Contact our support team for assistance with exchanges.

Payment

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We also offer buy now, pay later options through Klarna and Afterpay on qualifying orders.

Absolutely. EliteWear uses industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We are PCI-DSS compliant and never store full credit card numbers on our servers. Your security is our top priority.

Yes, we offer installment payment options through Klarna (4 interest-free payments) and Afterpay (bi-weekly installments). These options are available at checkout for orders up to $5,000. Subject to approval.

Yes, EliteWear offers digital and physical gift cards in denominations from $100 to $5,000. Digital gift cards are delivered instantly via email, while physical gift cards are shipped in a premium presentation box.

Products

Each product page includes a detailed size guide with measurements. We recommend taking your body measurements and comparing them to our size chart. If you need personalized sizing assistance, our style advisors are available via chat or email.

Every product on EliteWear is 100% authentic. We source directly from premium manufacturers and authorized distributors. Each item comes with a certificate of authenticity where applicable. We stand behind the quality and authenticity of every piece.

Each garment includes a care label with specific instructions. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for tailored pieces, hand washing for delicate fabrics, and storing items in a cool, dry place. Follow the care instructions to maintain your garment's quality.

Account

Click on the user icon in the top right corner and select "Create Account." Fill in your name, email address, and a secure password. You can also create an account during checkout. Once registered, you can access your dashboard, track orders, and manage your preferences.

On the login page, click "Forgot Password." Enter your email address, and we will send you a password reset link. The link expires within 24 hours. If you do not receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact support.

Log into your account and navigate to the Account Details section in your dashboard. Here you can update your name, email address, phone number, and password. Save your changes before leaving the page.

You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any marketing email. You can also update your communication preferences in your account settings under Notification Preferences.
