Frequently Asked Questions

We offer complimentary express shipping on all orders over $500. Standard shipping is $15 and takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is $25 and takes 2-3 business days. International shipping rates vary by destination.

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags attached. We provide a prepaid return label for domestic returns. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your order through your account dashboard or using the tracking link provided in the email.
